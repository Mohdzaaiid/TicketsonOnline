FAQ

Know Before You Groove

Where is the festival located?

In Brooklyn, NY – the exact venue location and entry details will be announced closer to the event date.

Are kids allowed?

Yes! Groovory is family-friendly during the day, up until 8 PM. After that, the festival becomes 16+.

Is the festival accessible?

Yes, the venue is wheelchair accessible and includes designated ADA restrooms and viewing areas.

What happens if it rains?

The festival runs rain or shine, so we recommend dressing for the forecast and planning accordingly.

Can I bring my own food or drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but don’t worry – we’ve got 40+ amazing food vendors on-site.

Do vendors offer vegetarian meals?

Yes! Many vendors offer delicious vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options throughout the festival.

Are pets allowed?

Sorry, no pets allowed – only trained service animals are permitted within the festival grounds for safety.

Is there parking available on-site?

Limited parking is available nearby, but we highly recommend using public transit or rideshare services.
